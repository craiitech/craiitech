# 🚀 Digital Transformation in Quality Assurance: Introducing the RSU EOMS Submission & Monitoring Portal!

We are proud to announce the official rollout of the **RSU EOMS Portal**, a state-of-the-art digital solution designed to elevate Romblon State University's commitment to academic excellence and institutional quality! 🎓🏛️

### 🌐 New Official Home
The portal has officially moved! You can now access the system at our new permanent domain:
👉 **[https://eoms.rsu.edu.ph](https://eoms.rsu.edu.ph)**

Developed through a strategic collaboration between the **Center for Research in Artificial Intelligence and Information Technologies (CRAIITech)** and the **Quality Assurance Office (QAO)**, this platform serves as the central nervous system for our Educational Organizations Management System (EOMS), aligned with **ISO 21001:2018** standards.

### 🌟 What makes the EOMS Portal a game-changer?

✅ **The Submission Hub:** A streamlined, revision-controlled workflow for all 6 core EOMS documents (SWOT, Needs & Expectations, Operational Plans, and more). No more lost emails—just pure, organized evidence!
✅ **Integrated Risk Register:** Units can now log, analyze, and monitor risks and opportunities in real-time, ensuring proactive institutional improvement.
✅ **IQA Strategic Planning:** Digital conduct of Internal Quality Audits with real-time evidence logging and automated summary reports.
✅ **Decision Support Analytics:** High-level dashboards for Administrators and Campus Directors to monitor maturity indices and institutional parity at a glance.
✅ **Formal Notices System:** Automated generation of Compliance and Non-Compliance notices to ensure university-wide documentation standards are met.

### 🤝 A Collaborative Milestone
This portal is a testament to the technical expertise of **CRAIITech** and the visionary leadership of the **Quality Assurance Office**. By digitizing our quality workflows, we aren't just moving to the cloud—we are strengthening our foundation for a smarter, more resilient university.

Stay tuned as we continue to innovate for a better RSU! 🔴⚪
